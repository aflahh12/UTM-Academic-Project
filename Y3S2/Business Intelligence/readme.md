# Business Intelligence

## Course Overview
This course introduces the concepts, technologies, and processes involved in transforming raw data into meaningful business insights for strategic decision-making. It covers data warehousing, ETL processes, dimensional modeling, OLAP, data visualization, dashboard design, and performance management using industry-standard BI tools.

## Reflection
This course gave me a comprehensive understanding of how business intelligence transforms raw data into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. I learned to design data warehouses, build interactive dashboards, and visualize key performance indicators that help organizations monitor and improve their operations. The practical BI skills I developed will be essential for bridging the gap between data and business strategy in my future career.
