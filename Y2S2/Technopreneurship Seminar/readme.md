# Technopreneurship Seminar

## Course Overview
This seminar explores the intersection of technology and entrepreneurship, focusing on how to identify opportunities, develop innovative tech-based business ideas, and bring them to market. It covers business model development, intellectual property, funding strategies, product commercialization, and the mindset required to succeed as a technology entrepreneur.
