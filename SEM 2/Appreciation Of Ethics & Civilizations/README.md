# Appreciation of Ethics & Civilizations

## Course Overview
This course examines the fundamental ethical theories and philosophical foundations that have shaped human civilizations across different cultures and time periods. It explores how moral principles, cultural values, and historical contexts influence individual behavior and societal development.

## Reflection
This course broadened my perspective on how ethical frameworks and cultural civilizations interact to shape human values and social structures. I gained a deeper appreciation for diverse worldviews and the importance of ethical reasoning in addressing complex societal challenges. The comparative study of civilizations taught me that understanding historical and cultural contexts is essential for fostering mutual respect and thoughtful decision-making in our interconnected world.
